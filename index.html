Fire & Acoustics

Fire Rating Upto 120 Minutes

Fire safety is at the top of everyones agenda, we are one of very few who can manufacture doors with up to 120 minutes fire rating meaning that if the worst were to happen you can be reassured that you are protected.

42dB Acoustic Rating

Whether it be for a hotel or just a door in your home, noise penetration is key to being completely satisfied with your door. With acoustic ratings of up to 42dB you can be assured a good nights sleep.

The Finishing Touches

Ironmongery & Steel Inlays

Ironmongery is the key to finishing your door right. Our supply chain covers traditional and modern designs made from iron, brass or many other finishes ensuring a finish that fits in perfectly, or why not style the door with a polished inlay?

Polished, Stained or Waxed

If the natural wood look isn't quite what you're after then our finishing team offer colour matching to your requirements so the timber can be stained to match your surroundings, or a wax to give it that extra dimension. Whatever you need we will ensure your needs are met.

The Extra Moulding Detail

The moulding detail can make the door, no matter if you are matching up to an existing profile within the building or just want that little bit of extra detail, we can design and machine either a beading for the face or an architrave to frame the door to make sure it's finished right.
